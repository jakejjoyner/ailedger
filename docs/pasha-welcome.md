You're being brought on as a 1099 sales contractor. Your job: take AILedger out to real companies that need EU AI Act compliance (and similar emerging regulations in the US) and close them.

Details on rates, scope, and term are in the MSA/SOW you're signing. If any of it feels off, push back before you sign. After you sign it's awkward to unwind.
