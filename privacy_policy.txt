Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 11, 2026

1. Information we collect
Account email address. Billing information (processed by Stripe - we do not store card data). AI inference metadata: model name, provider, latency, status code, timestamp, and SHA-256 hashes of inputs/outputs.

2. Information we do not collect
Raw AI inputs and/or outputs are never stored. We cannot reconstruct what was sent to and/or received from any AI provider.

3. Legal basis (GDPR)
We process your data to perform the Terms of Service agreement you have with us (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) and to comply with financial record-keeping obligations (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR).

4. Data residency
All data is stored in the EU, namely AWS eu-central-1 (Frankfurt, Germany) via Supabase. This applies to all plans.

5. Retention
Inference logs are append-only and retained indefinitely by design - this is core to the compliance value of the service. Account data is deleted upon request.

6. Your rights
EU residents have the right to access, rectify, and erase personal data. Contact support@ailedger.dev to exercise these rights. Note: inference log hashes cannot be deleted as they contain no personal data.
